I'm Seth

Hello my Name is Seth Walton, and I graduated from ITT-tech for Computer Electronics and Engineering Technology in 2005. I worked as a Teachers Aid in computer programing both years I was in school. The class was mainly Visual Basic which I had previous experience in High School. I went to work Immediately after graduation for National Instruments in their Research And Development team. I worked on Pxi/Vxi/Rio controllers mainly. After NI I moved back to Kansas and started doing electronic repair work and Network repair for local businesses when I had time. So I started my own side business called Walton Electronics.

I Have been doing the In home electronic repair ever since. In my spare time I have been learning new technologies and circuit designs. I have also just completed KU Coding Program.
